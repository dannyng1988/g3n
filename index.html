Product

Bailey

Introducing Bailey, our cutting-edge semi-humanoid robot, powered by a sophisticated Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model. Bailey represents a significant leap forward in robotics, effectively unifying perception, language understanding, and learned control. This integration enables Bailey to perform a wide range of tasks with remarkable proficiency.
